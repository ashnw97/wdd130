Overview

Purpose

My website will help make adoption easy for individuals looking to find a feline companion or two or three! I want my website to show pictures of the fuzzy candidates and give a little information about each one, give the viewers education and statistics surrounding cats, and make the adoption process as easy as possible so that nothing stands in the way of these beautiful creatures finding homes.

Audience

My audience are adults (as young as 18) who are looking to adopt a furry friend. My audience is also those who are on the fence about adopting cats or unsure of what it is like to own a cat.
