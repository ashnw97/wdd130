Sorry Rapids

The Sorry Rapids can be found in the south of Board Game Valley. It is a river that spans 20 miles and contains 4 different rapids. The river has beautiful scenery with 2 waterfalls within view of the float. You can count on witnessing more than one hundred different fish species, more than 50 species of birds, and much more. These rapids are sought out by thrill-lovers and adrenaline-seekers everywhere.

The Catan

A river that runs along the pacific northwest, the Catan is gorgeous all year around. Hidden amongst lush landscapes and tall mountains this river also houses supposed local legend "Great Ghengis" who has haunted fisherman for decades. A distance of 32.7 miles makes this waterway a perfect destination for adventure-seekers with plenty of areas for rests and stops along the way.

Boggle River

The Boggle River has crystal clear water all year around. At peak day you can see a crisp reflection of yourself as you gaze into it. At night, however, you can see bioluminescent crawdads illuminating the night, making for an eerie, yet brilliant sight. With 10.2 miles of smooth waterways, it is a great destination for families and couples who wish to spend some time away from the troubles and noises of everyday life.
